About TECHNOVATION

TECHNOVATION 2K25 is the flagship technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Mailam Engineering College. This event brings together innovative minds, tech enthusiasts, and industry experts to showcase talent, share knowledge, and foster innovation.

This one-day extravaganza features technical and non-technical events including paper presentations, project exhibitions, Ideathons, workshops, and fun activities designed to challenge your skills and creativity.
